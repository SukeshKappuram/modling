Active Users

The platform has more than 1.5 billion active users globally. India has the largest user base with 43% new users.

Growth

The first quarter of 2019 witnessed Tik Tok being downloaded by almost 108 million new users worldwide - a growth of 70% from the first quarter of 2018.

Downloads

On Apple’s iOS App store the Tik Tok App was the most downloaded App in Q1 of 2019 – 33 million downloads. Even so, the Android version is more in use than the iOS version.

Interesting demographic research proves that almost 60% of active Tik Tok users are aged between 16 and 24 years.
